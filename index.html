Agility FC

Join the 'agility fc' friends chat (FC) in game to run laps at the Wilderness Agility Course with the hive.

The FC is neither a clan nor a service. It is an open community for members to organize and agree on some common rules.

  • We run on Bounty Hunter worlds — 318, 319, and 569.
  • No regular schedule; masses happen through member initiative.
  • Check Discord for mega masses: Announcement Channel
  • How to check: Look for a pattern in the in-game FC list (sort by world) or scout the course.
  • Course is in Level 50+ Wilderness. Come at your own risk.
  • Keep Private chat on 'Friends' or 'Off'.
  • Heed the calls of leadership: officers, mods, and admins.
